Student Work > Video

For this Sound Design project, students were tasked with selecting a 30-second video file and editing different ambient sound and music with it to change it to three different moods.

From a pre-determined list of silent movie clips, Sound Design students were tasked with matching audio to their selected scene. Using primarily stock audio clips and music, students created a mix that complemented their scene and created the desired mood.

For this project, Sound Design students were required to storyboard, shoot, and then edit a silent story. Then, using Foley recordings of their own, they had to add all of the necessary audio to create a compelling finished product.

As the final project in Sound Design, students storyboarded, shot, and then edited a story of their own design. The finished project was required to use a majority of the skills learned throughout the semester such as: J-Cuts and L-Cuts, EQ, Diegetic/Non-Diegetic/Trans-Diegetic Audio, Sound Effects Editing, and Music Mixing.

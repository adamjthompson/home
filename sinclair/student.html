Student Work

The designs on these page are those of my Visual Communications and Digital Media Design students at Sinclair Community College. While I cannot claim direct credit from the designs shown, I believe that the quality of their work speaks volumes of the design instruction and critique they received during my classes.

I have divided the work into Print, Web, and Video categories. The print and web designs shown are generally from the first or second semester of a two-year degree program and many of the students have no graphic design or software skills when beginning their studies. The video projects are generally from second-year students.

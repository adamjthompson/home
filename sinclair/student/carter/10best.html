10 BEST WEBSITES

deadlegacy.com
Site Goal(s)- Provide quality apparel and promote individuality.
Target- Adults 25-30, more “street” crowd.
Navigation- Moderate, gets clumpy.
Usability- Pretty easy, though the many categories start to jumble together as you start to get specific.
Content- Sells typical apparel like shirts and hoodies with either individualistic designs or name-brand simple quality to premium
outdoor jackets & coats.

etq-amsterdam.com
Site Goal(s)- Provide high quality shoes with style and last a long time
Target- Adults aged 23-27, mainly male, (though they do provide shoes for women), simplistic lifestyle.
Navigation- Very simple and to the point.
Usability- Easy to use, navigation is a breeze, no clumpy categories.
Content- Sells high-quality (and expensive) shoes in variety of types in a minimalistic style.

huru.rocks
Site Goal(s)- Promote high quality backpacks with functional purposes.
Target- Unisex, androgynous audience aged 19-24, modern
Navigation- Easy, simple scrolling technique.
Usability- Simple to use with few categories to choose from, continuous scrolling provides more information on the product.
Content- Sells backpacks with multifunctional purposes and provides additional information on the various ways to use the products.

larkshead.com
Site Goal(s)- Promote caring for the environment and human connection through apparel.
Target- Promote caring for the environment and human connection through apparel.
Navigation- Easy to navigate through.
Usability- Very easy, straight-forward buttons.
Content-Sells apparel such as laid back clothing like hoodies and sweatpants along with accessories such as customized patches.

nikitaclothing.com
Site Goal(s)- To provide clothing for women who sled and ski mountains frequently.
Target- Young adult women 20-25 years old who do activity in cold weather.
Navigation- Moderate, simple.
Usability- Easy, but pages look almost identical which can easily confuse.
Content- Sells winter or mountain apparel/accessories like heavy jackets and heavy hoodies, crewnecks, and beanies.

pand.co
Site Goal(s)- Promote adventurous lifestyle through apparel.
Target- Adults 25-35 years old, “creative” and adventurous audience like tattoo artists, skaters, surfers, people who “want to make a statement”.
Navigation- Easy.
Usability- Easy, but lots of categories to chose from that can get confusing.
Content- Typical apparel like shirts and hoodies but also includes leather & denim jackets.

rottefella.no
Site Goal(s)- Promote people to get active in all weather through weather protected clothing.
Target- Adults aged 28-25, athletic.
Navigation- Easy, straight-forward.
Usability- Straight-forward usability, easy to find pages.
Content- Sells thermal sportswear and provides information on how products are made and what they are made of.

tinkerwatches.com
Site Goal(s)- Provide consumers to personalize watches to an individual’s tastes while keeping a timeless look.
Target- Adults aged 28-35, male, businessmen.
Navigation- Fairly easy.
Usability- Very easy, very informative, very creative as well.
Content- Allows users to design watches from clock size to color to wrist size and finish.

voltafootwear.com
Site Goal(s)- Promote shoes that encourage a classic and simple style.
Target- Adult men aged 26-34, fashion-forward.
Navigation- Fairly simple.
Usability- Easy, very simple, straight forward, and no cluster of buttons.
Content- Sells high-quality shoes along with hoodies featuring graphic designs.

yelvy.com
Site Goal(s)- Promote unique styles of clothing and unique people.
Target- Teenagers aged 17+ and young adults aged 20-23, a more “hipster” audience.
Navigation- Unique, can kind of trip you out but overall very cool.
Usability- Scrolling is tricky and certain buttons are placed oddly, but overall easy to use after looking around website.
Content- Provides just t-shirts, winter coats, dad hats, and backpacks at the moment but have unique graphic design elements.

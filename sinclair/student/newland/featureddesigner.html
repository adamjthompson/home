Sparks of inspiration

about

Amber Brannon. A designer full of love and creativity. Amber is listed in the top ten designers in Tennessee and along with that was salutatorian at her college. She has accomplished so much working with large companies and big names like Taylor Swift.

In 2016 she spread her wings and decided to quit her nine to five job and start her own company. As she said herself she couldn't quite find that job she had been dreaming so she created. Then came along Copperheart Creative. Amber, the founder and creative director of her own company where she continues to build up her business the way she wants to. Taking charge and taking names is the Amber Brannon way and an inspiring one too. Ambers designs are clean and playful. A well-rounded designer with that much passion nothing can stand in your way!

Not only is Mrs.Brannon a designer but with that comes her heartful blogs. Her blogs keep her connected and there always full of inspiring words for all. She gets personal and that's all the more reason clients want to work with her.

The style is key and hers is a key. She makes everything look simple, organized and cutting edge. If you take a look at some of her logo designs you can see how elegant and open each one is. With a light color palette and tasteful typography, how could you go wrong.

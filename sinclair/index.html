About

This is a basic responsive web site that I put together to demonstrate my level of proficiency with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. While it does not, perhaps, encompass everything that I can do, it should at least demonstrate that I possess an intermediate level of knowledge in web design.

Currently, the focus of my teaching responsibilities has been in the field of video production and motion design, but I also have a background in freelance web design and some print design as well.

Fun fact: The maze texture used on this web site and in my other application materials is from a maze I created as a youth. I used to LOVE creating mazes and gifting them to family and friends, and I had a reputation of creating very challenging mazes. It's a hobby I think I'd like to pick up again sometime.

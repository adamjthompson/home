Student Work

The designs on these page are those of my own Visual Communications students at Sinclair Community College. While I cannot claim direct credit from the designs shown, I believe that the quality of their work speaks volumes of the design instruction and critique they received during my classes.

The designs shown are generally from the first or second semester of a two-year degree program and many of the students have no graphic design or software skills when beginning their studies.

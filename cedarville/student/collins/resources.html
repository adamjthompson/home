Search Engine Optimization

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) uses keywords and phrases to get your site found in search engines and algorithms, and also helps Google know what your site is about. SEO helps maximize traffic and website visits. Good use of SEO will keep your website higher up on lists of results of search engines.

Using specific keywords with different variations helps your site get found in searches. And other factors like external links, and sites that link to yours will effect how engines like Google find your site. Connections to sites with good reputation will also reflect well on your website. The content within your site along with titles also come up in search engines and can affect how often your website will appear on searches. There are many factors of SEO, it may seem complex but with a good understanding of how searches work and web design you can get your site higher up in rankings.

Cascading Style Sheets

Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) is a style sheet language that defines styles and presentation of web pages and markup medias like HTML. It affects how the site runs, looks, and can affect how it appears in search results. CSS is primarily used for the presentation, so it is important in making an aesthetically pleasing and useful website for its users.

CSS is used to change fonts, colors, styles, add content, and for more advanced designs like responsive elements and animation. Without CSS a website is very basic. HTML can also do some of these things but not as effectively. An advantage of CSS is that you can create an external style sheet, and the CSS rules you create will apply to any page thats connected to the style sheet. CSS makes it fast and easy to make an appealing design.

Hosting Websites

Website hosting is a service that allows individuals and businesses to publish a website on the internet. The website is hosted or stored onto computers called servers. There are multiple ways to host a site, and they have different advantages and disadvantages including budget, security, and versatility.

Hosting a website yourself requires a dedicated computer server. Self hosting can have issues like power outages, maintenance, and slow connections. Professional hosting won’t be effected by power outages, fast connections, professional help with maintenance, and a static IP address.

There are many options, and knowing what your site needs will help you decide what is the best hosting for your website. Shared hosting means your site shares a server with other sites, dedicated is one server hosting yours alone, VPS hosting uses multiple servers to host your website.

Designing A Website

Website design is important, it affects usability, search results, and other aspects that will keep visitors or drive them away. When making a website you must know the site’s target audience, visual hierarchy, color palette, visual aesthetic. A great site design can keep visitors and make your business look professional and credible.

People scan websites to quickly find information, so your site must be legible and have a design that leads the eye through your content with ease. Websites should be self-explanatory and not require the user to work hard for what they are looking for. Having a difficult design, or things like pop-up windows can cause users to leave your site. Sites must also strive to be unobtrusive and patient with users and not force them to give out information or make accounts before they can see their content.

Usability Considerations

Website usability is an important aspect of your design, and helps bring multiple audiences to your site. The website needs to be consistent, logical, accessible, legible, and user-centric. Things like illegible type, broken links, and bad responsiveness can give your site a negative reputation and plummet traffic.

Inconveniences like serves being down, broken links, and other issues can drive users away. Especially on sites that have frequent traffic. Making sure your site is always available and usable makes it look professional and usable. Sites should also strive to walk new users through the site so they can become familiar with it. Your site needs to be simple, legible, responsive, and functional on multiple platforms like mobile devices, tablets, and TVs. A familiar, credible, and accessible site will keep users coming back.
